About Us

The most prominent feature of our website is disease detection by analyzing the symptoms that you input. Once a potential disease has been identified, the website will then provide a referral service to help users connect with doctors who specialize in treating that particular condition. Overall, such a website could be a valuable resource for people who are experiencing health problems and looking for guidance on next steps.
